About Us

Supporting Families through Compassionate Autism Education

Established in 2005, Essential Autism provides educational support and services to people of all ages who have a developmental disability. We specialize in Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA), delivering services to families and service providers in Windsor-Essex and the surrounding region. Our skilled team provides supports in-home and in-community, based on the specific needs of the individual.

Step's To Finding The Perfect Match

At Essential Autism, we believe in a 3-step process to finding the perfect match for each family we call the 3-M's.

MEET

Initiate your journey with a personal meeting where we engage with clients and families to conduct comprehensive assessments. This session helps us understand individual needs and design personalized therapy plans.

MATCH

Foster a meaningful bond and connection between individuals and their dedicated support workers. Our team prioritizes building trust and understanding to ensure effective collaboration, personalized care, and ensuring they achieve their goals with confidence.

MEMORIES

Create lasting memories of progress and achievement. Whether it's celebrating achievements, overcoming challenges, or enjoying moments of growth, our team is dedicated to fostering positive experiences that enrich lives for families and individuals alike.
