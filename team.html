Meet The Founder and CEO


Dwayne Wessel

Dwayne Wessel is a dedicated Clinical Supervisor in Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA). He is passionate about supporting individuals with autism and developmental disabilities, working closely with therapists and technicians to ensure the highest standards of care. Dwayne’s collaborative approach empowers both clients and staff to achieve their goals.

Dwayne is committed to developing personalized treatment plans that address the unique needs of each individual. With a focus on improving quality of life, his mission is to create meaningful, positive change for the individuals and families he serves at Essential Autism.
