About Us

Supporting Families through Compassionate Autism Education

Established in 2005, Essential Autism provides educational support and services to people of all ages who have a developmental disability. We specialize in Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA), delivering services to families and service providers in Windsor-Essex and the surrounding region. Our skilled team provides supports in-home and in-community, based on the specific needs of the individual.

Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to your questions about autism support, educational services, and our commitment to families.

What types of support services do you offer?

We specialize in providing Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy and Respite Services. Our ABA therapy programs are tailored to promote positive behavior changes and skill development in individuals with autism spectrum disorder (ASD). Additionally, we offer respite services to support families by providing temporary relief and care for their loved ones.

How can I begin services with Essential Autism?

Fill out our contact form with your information, and our team will reach out to you promptly to schedule a consultation or meeting. We look forward to discussing how Essential Autism can support you and your family.

What age groups do you provide services for?

Essential Autism offers services for individuals of all ages, from early childhood through adulthood. Our programs are tailored to meet the developmental and educational needs of each age group, ensuring comprehensive support throughout their journey.
