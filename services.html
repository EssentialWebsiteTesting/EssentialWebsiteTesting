The Behavioral Approach

Applied Behavior Analysis

At Essential Autism, our Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) services are delivered through a close collaborative effort between Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and dedicated technicians. But what exactly is ABA? It’s a scientifically validated method that focuses on improving specific behaviors while teaching new skills. Our team emphasizes the importance of data collection, utilizing real-time information to track progress and adjust strategies to ensure optimal outcomes for each child. This individualized approach allows us to tailor interventions to meet the unique needs of every child, fostering skill development and enhancing their quality of life.

Support for Families

Respite Services

Understanding the challenges families face, Essential Autism offers comprehensive respite services designed to provide caregivers with a much-needed break. But what is respite? It is a temporary relief that allows families to recharge while ensuring that their loved ones receive quality care. We offer respite in both home and office settings, allowing families to choose what suits them best. Our trained technicians provide compassionate support, creating a safe environment where individuals can thrive. This unique combination of flexibility and understanding sets our respite services apart, making a positive impact on families’ overall well-being.

Training and Speaking

Navigating Resources

Fostering Inclusivity

Community Involvement

Essential Autism actively engages with the community, creating inclusive environments for children with autism to connect and socialize. Our weekly gaming groups, featuring popular platforms like Minecraft and Roblox, provide opportunities for children to interact with peers in a fun and supportive setting. Led by our trained technicians, these online groups allow children to build friendships while developing essential social skills. By fostering these connections, Essential Autism stands out as an organization dedicated to not just therapy, but also creating a sense of community and belonging.
